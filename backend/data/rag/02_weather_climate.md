## Monsoon and rice

Delayed or weak monsoon rains can stress transplant and nurseries, supporting paddy quotes. Excess rain and floods risk field losses and quality downgrades near harvest.

## Drought and heat

Prolonged heat raises irrigation demand and can cut yields for rainfed crops. Heat waves also disrupt vegetable logistics and shorten shelf life.

## Cyclones and storms

Coastal storms can damage storage, roads, and ports, temporarily tightening supply and raising replacement costs for traders.

## Temperature trends

Warmer nights and shifting seasonality affect pest pressure and planting calendars, which traders sometimes price ahead of official production estimates.
